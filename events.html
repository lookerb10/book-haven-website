Regular Programs

Book Club

When: Second Thursday of every month at 6:30 PM

Where: Community Room

Join our friendly book club for lively discussions about contemporary literature. Each month we select a new book and explore themes, characters, and the author's craft. New members always welcome!

Current Read: "Educated" by Tara Westover

Children's Story Time

When: Every Saturday at 2:00 PM

Where: Kids Corner

Bring your little ones for an interactive story time featuring classic and new children's books. We include songs, activities, and sometimes special guest readers. Perfect for ages 3-8.

Poetry Night

When: Third Friday of every month at 7:00 PM

Where: Main Reading Area

Share your original poetry or listen to others read their work. This supportive open mic environment welcomes poets of all skill levels. Sign-up starts 30 minutes before the event.
